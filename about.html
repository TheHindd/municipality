About KKTC Municipality

One of the most significant coastal towns in the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus and the Mediterranean Sea is Famagusta. The city is a major hub for education and tourism as well. The Lusignan era saw the majority of Famagusta's development, and the city is home to several historical relics from the Latin Kingdoms, the Venetians, the Turks, and the Roman and Eastern Roman Empires. The Venetian Palace, Othello Castle, St. Barnabas Monastery, Lala Mustafa Pasha Mosque (St. Nicholas Cathedral), and golden beaches are a few of these.
